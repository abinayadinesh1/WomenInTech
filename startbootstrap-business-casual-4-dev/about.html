About: Problems, Prejudice, and Partiality

As ladies, whether we are tall or short, young or old, rich or poor, we all have one thing in common. We have all been stereotyped before. No matter where we are, it seems to be in peoples' biology to judge us on our physical appearence, gender, or race. I think it's time we changed that.

Here, people who are unaware of the difficulties and prejudice women face can try to understand the struggles we go through in our everyday lives.
