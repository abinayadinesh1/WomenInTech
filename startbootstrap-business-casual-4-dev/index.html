By: Abinaya Dinesh

Live a life worth living

When you were younger, what did you want to be when you grew up?

Most children answer along the lines of "astronaut", or "scientist", or "artist".

When you got a little bit older, about the time of middle school, what did you want to be then?

Some might have said, "I want to be a lawyer,or doctor, or businesswoman.

At the time, you probably thought that this course of action would be the best for you. But how were you to know that the world didn't just allow these things to happen without some troubles along the way? This mostly involves the people around you, who probably don't believe that a person like you could achieve such a feat. At times, you may feel like there's no hope, or that nobody believes in you, and that might even be true! But the most important thing you can do for yourself is try. If you can ignore all of the people around you, and do everything in your power to acheive your goal, you have already suceeded.

But don't get me wrong, cutting yourself off from other people is also not the right way to go. Being able to work in a team, and being able to adapt to any type of environment are also important skills that you probably use in everyday life. You just have to find tthe perfect middle. Block out the people who bring you down, and seek help from the people who believe you can make a difference.
